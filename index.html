Thinking Aloud

Graduation Project — Royal College of Art

Thinking Aloud captures the subconscious behaviours people make while thinking, and reads them as a measure of cognitive work. By giving that invisible mental effort a tangible value, the project eases the anxiety that arises when work feels stuck or shows no visible output.
